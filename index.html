Zhiyi Zhang

Ph.D. Candidate @ UCLA

    I am a third year Ph.D. candidate of CS department at UCLA, under the supervision of Prof. Lixia Zhang. I am now a Graduate Student Researcher in the Internet Research Laboratory (IRL). My main research interests are Named Data Networking (NDN) and Network Security. My previous work done in NDN security includes Name-based Access Control, Attribute-based Access Control over NDN, NDN Certificate Management and DDoS Defense over NDN. I am also contributing to the development of the core NDN softwares, including NDN Forwarding Daemon (NFD) and ndn-cxx library.
    I received my Bachelor of Engineering in Software Engineering from Nankai University, China, in 2016. While pursuing my bachelor degree, I was an undergraduate student research member in the Data and Knowledge Engineering Laboratory of Nankai University.
